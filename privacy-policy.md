# Privacy Policy — Qibla Compass

**Last updated:** April 25, 2026

## Overview
Qibla Compass is a free mobile application that helps users find the direction of the Kaaba (Qibla) using GPS and compass sensor. This privacy policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

---

## Data We Collect

### Location Data
- The app requests access to your device's GPS location **only** to calculate the Qibla direction and the distance to Mecca.
- Location data is **not stored**, **not shared**, and **not transmitted** to any server.
- Location is processed entirely on your device.

### Device Sensors
- The app uses the device compass/orientation sensor to rotate the needle.
- Sensor data is **not stored** or **transmitted** anywhere.

### Advertising
- This app displays advertisements provided by **Google AdMob**.
- AdMob may collect and use your **Advertising ID** to show personalized ads.
- For more information, see [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

---

## Data We Do NOT Collect
- We do not collect your name, email, or any personal information.
- We do not create user accounts.
- We do not store your location history.
- We do not sell any data to third parties.

---

## Third-Party Services
- **Google AdMob** — Advertising ([Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy))
- **Nominatim / OpenStreetMap** — Used for reverse geocoding (city name lookup). No personal data is stored. ([Privacy Policy](https://osmfoundation.org/wiki/Privacy_Policy))
